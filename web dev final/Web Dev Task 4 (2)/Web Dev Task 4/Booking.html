FAQs

Find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our Park. If you don't see your question here, feel free to contact us for more information.

Yes, for the safety of our guests, certain rides have height and age restrictions. Each ride will have clearly posted signs indicating the requirements. You can also check the restrictions on our website or at the entrance of each ride. If you have any specific concerns, our staff at the park are always available to help.

For the safety and enjoyment of all our guests, outside food and drinks are not permitted in the park. However, we offer a variety of dining options within the park to cater to all tastes and dietary needs. Exceptions are made for baby food and guests with specific dietary requirements or medical conditions. Please contact guest services for more details.

Our water park is easily accessible by public transport. You can take bus number X from the city center directly to our entrance. The nearest train station is Y, from which you can take a short bus ride or taxi to the park. Detailed directions and public transport schedules are available on our website.

In the event of an emergency, please remain calm and follow the instructions of our trained staff. Emergency exits are clearly marked throughout the park. First aid stations are also available, and our medical team is ready to assist with any health concerns. If you notice any suspicious activity or require immediate assistance, please inform the nearest staff member.

Yes, we strive to make our park accessible to all guests. We offer a range of services for visitors with disabilities, including accessible entrances, wheelchair rentals, and designated viewing areas for shows and attractions. Our staff is trained to assist guests with special needs. For more detailed information, please visit our accessibility services page or contact guest services prior to your visit.
