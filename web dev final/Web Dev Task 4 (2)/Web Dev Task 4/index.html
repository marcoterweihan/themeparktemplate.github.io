Types of Rides

Dive into excitement with our diverse selection of water rides! From thrilling slides to relaxing rivers, we have something for everyone.

Great White Swing

Get ready to soar through the air on the Great White Swing! This shark-themed attraction offers a thrilling swing ride that propels you high above the water, giving you the sensation of being chased by the ocean's apex predator.

Pirate's Plunge

Embark on Pirate's Plunge! Experience thrilling drops, twists, and splashes through pirate battles and hidden treasure caves. Adventure awaits on the high seas!

Rainbow Slides

Experience zooming through our expertly-engineered Rainbow Slides at record speeds!

Lazy Lagoon

Come rest & relax at the Lazy Lagoon after an adrenaline-filled afternoon at the Water Park!

Buccaneer Blitz

Race down the Buccaneer Blitz slide! Compete with friends through twists, turns, and splashes in a high-speed, thrilling water adventure. Ready, set, slide!

Exceptions are made for baby food and guests with specific dietary requirements or medical conditions. Please contact guest services for more details.

Our water park is easily accessible by public transport. You can take bus number X from the city center directly to our entrance. In the event of an emergency, please remain calm and follow the instructions of our trained staff. Emergency exits are clearly marked throughout the park. First aid stations are also available, and our medical team is ready to assist with any health concerns. If you notice any suspicious activity or require immediate assistance, please inform the nearest staff member.
